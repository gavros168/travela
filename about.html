Our Story

Why Travela exists

Travela Sdn. Bhd. was set up in Cheras, Kuala Lumpur to serve distributors, retailers and online sellers who need a single, reliable source for travel goods — not a different supplier for every product line. Rather than sourcing suitcases from one factory, straps and tags from another and winter accessories from a third, we carry luggage bags, suitcases, travel pouches, luggage straps, travel vacuum bags, travel pillows, hand carry bungee cords, bag tags, luggage scales, passport cases, padlocks, umbrellas, scarves, winter gloves and winter socks under a single wholesale account.

Our registered business nature covers wholesale, general trading, distribution, retail and e-commerce — which means we work with the full chain of buyers in the travel goods trade: bulk wholesale buyers, regional distributors, retail shopfronts and online resellers, each on terms suited to their order volume and channel.

Every listing in our catalog is priced and sold per unit, with specifications and dimensions confirmed before dispatch. That's also why our quotes reflect real stock counts and real lead times, not a marked-up catalog estimate.

Common Questions

Before you send an enquiry

The questions we get asked most by distributors, retailers and online sellers.

What's your minimum order quantity?

Minimum order quantities vary by product line and will be confirmed at the quotation stage based on real stock on hand.

Can I mix product lines in one order?

Yes. Because all six category groups run through the same wholesale account, you can combine luggage, accessories and weather goods into a single purchase order and one delivery.

Do you supply retailers as well as wholesalers?

Yes. Our registered business nature covers wholesale, distribution, retail and e-commerce, so we can work with bulk buyers, regional distributors, retail shopfronts and online resellers on terms suited to each.

Do you ship outside Malaysia?

Yes. As part of our general trading activity we arrange international shipments by air, sea or courier, confirmed at the quotation stage.
