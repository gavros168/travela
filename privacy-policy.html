01 Data Controller

The data controller responsible for personal data described in this policy is Travela Sdn. Bhd., registered in Malaysia (Registration No. 202601026468 / 1688564-X), with its registered address at 8-6-20, Queens Avenue, Jalan Bayam, 55100 Cheras, W.P. Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.

02 Personal Data We Collect

Depending on how you interact with us, we may collect: your full name; company or store name; email address; phone number; billing and delivery addresses; order details including product categories and quantities; payment reference information (we do not store full card numbers); and any other information you choose to include in an enquiry or purchase order.

03 How We Collect Data

We collect personal data directly from you when you submit our website enquiry form, email or call us, issue a purchase order, or otherwise communicate with us in the course of a business relationship. We do not purchase personal data from third-party data brokers or list vendors.

04 Purpose of Processing

We use personal data to: prepare and send quotations; process, pack and fulfil orders; confirm product specifications and quantities before dispatch; arrange shipping and delivery; issue invoices and process payments; respond to enquiries and provide customer support; and, where you have given consent, send updates about new stock or seasonal collections. We do not use personal data for automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects on individuals.

05 Legal Basis Under the PDPA

We process personal data on the basis that you have provided it voluntarily for the purpose of obtaining a quotation, placing an order, or otherwise engaging our services, which constitutes consent under the PDPA, and because processing is necessary to perform a contract with you or to take steps at your request before entering into one. Where we rely on consent for optional communications, such as marketing updates, you may withdraw that consent at any time as described in Clause 10.

06 Sharing With Third Parties

We share personal data only where necessary to fulfil your order or as required by law, including with: courier and freight companies, to arrange delivery; and our accounting and banking service providers, to process payment and issue invoices. We require these parties to handle personal data securely and only for the purpose for which it was shared. We do not sell personal data to third parties for their own marketing purposes.

07 Cross-Border Transfer

Where an order or general trading arrangement involves a delivery address, courier or supplier located outside Malaysia, personal data such as a delivery address or recipient contact detail may be transferred outside Malaysia solely for the purpose of completing that shipment. Where this occurs, we take reasonable steps to ensure the receiving party affords the personal data a standard of protection comparable to that required under the PDPA.

08 Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose it was collected for, to satisfy accounting and tax record-keeping obligations under Malaysian law (generally seven years from the relevant transaction), and to resolve any dispute or defend a legal claim. Enquiry data that does not result in an order is retained for a reasonable period to allow follow-up, after which it is deleted or anonymized if no further engagement occurs.

09 Data Security

We apply reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure or destruction, including restricting access to customer records to staff who need it to perform their role. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and while we work to protect personal data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

10 Your Rights

Under the PDPA, you have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, to request correction of inaccurate data, and to withdraw any consent you previously gave for us to process your data for a particular purpose, including marketing communications. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in Clause 14. We will respond within a reasonable time and in accordance with the requirements of the PDPA, noting that withdrawing consent may affect our ability to continue providing services that depend on that data.

11 Cookies & Website Analytics

This website does not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies. It may use strictly necessary technical functions required for the site to display correctly, including your cart contents, which are stored only in your own browser. If we introduce analytics or marketing cookies in the future, this section will be updated to describe them and, where required, to obtain your consent before they are set.

12 Children's Privacy

Our services are directed at businesses and adult individuals acting in a business capacity, such as distributors, retailers and online sellers. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children, and any personal data submitted to us is expected to belong to an adult authorized to act on behalf of their organization.

13 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our data practices or legal obligations. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
